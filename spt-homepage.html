Your Journey Ahead

You’ll complete 3 phases of this program:

Phase 1

Core Curriculum

Complete our 12-week core curriculum (aligned to HSPA’s CRCST exam). Then, you’ll head into Study Weeks before taking the CRCST exam.

Phase 2

Exam Prep and Pass

Head into Study Weeks, when you'll complete practice exams and review material, before sitting for the real CRCST exam.

Phase 3

Hands-On Training to Full Certification

Secure a full-time, paid training role in a healthcare setting. After you pass the CRCST exam and complete 400 training hours, you’ll be eligible for certification.
