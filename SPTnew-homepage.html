Your Path

A journey designed around you

Three connected phases take you from day one to a recognized credential and a paid role in a healthcare setting.

1
Phase 1 · Weeks 1–12

Core Curriculum

Complete a 12-week core curriculum aligned to HSPA's CRCST exam, with weekly live review sessions and a dedicated instructor.

2
Phase 2 · Study Weeks

Exam Prep & Pass

Move into focused Study Weeks with practice exams and targeted review, then sit for the official CRCST exam with confidence.

3
Phase 3 · Certification

Hands-On Training to Full Certification

Secure a full-time, paid training role. After passing the exam and completing 400 training hours, you're eligible for certification.
